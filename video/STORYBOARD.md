# ASPYR Athlete Announcement — Storyboard & Prompt Pack

30-second cinematic announcement. Vertical 9:16 (1080×1920), 30 fps.
Feed the per-shot prompts to Runway Gen-3 / Sora / Veo / Kling.

## Concept
Calm → contrast (endurance vs. strength) → brand reveal. Natural light,
minimal palette, slight film grain, DSLR look. Calm, confident tone.
Subject: 24M, athletic build, blonde curly hair, either shirtless or in
navy ASPYR tee.

## Shot list

### Shot 1 — Opening (0.0 – 4.0s)
Medium close-up. Subject sitting or standing still, looking off-camera.
Soft window light, warm. Camera barely moves (slow push-in ~5%).
Slight shallow depth of field. No dialogue. Natural ambience.

### Shot 2 — Running (4.0 – 8.5s)
Wide to medium tracking shot. Subject running on a forest path or
seaside road at golden hour. Backlit. Wind in hair, steady breath
audible. Smooth gimbal/dolly feel. Subject shirtless OR in navy ASPYR
tee (pick one and keep consistent per shot).

### Shot 3 — Gym lift (8.5 – 13.0s)
Dark gym, single rim light. Controlled barbell deadlift or pull-up —
one rep, full tension. Sweat visible. Low camera angle, slight dutch.
Shallow DOF. Shirtless.

### Shot 4 — Close-ups (13.0 – 17.0s)
Three micro-beats cut tight:
  a) Hands chalking up (1.3s)
  b) Breath / chest rise, low key light (1.3s)
  c) Eyes — focus, steady (1.4s)

### Shot 5 — Still moment (17.0 – 21.0s)
Subject standing, arms loose, head slightly down then lifts. Shallow
DOF. Whisper of wind or room tone. Locked-off frame. Shirtless.

### Shot 6 — ASPYR reveal (21.0 – 24.0s)
Match cut from shirtless to navy ASPYR tee in same pose. Tee logo
clearly readable, centered. Cool neutral light, slight moodiness.

### Shot 7 — Hero portrait (24.0 – 28.0s)
3/4 angle portrait in ASPYR tee. Subject looks down then slowly toward
lens. Soft key, hard edge shadow on opposite side. Frame leaves
negative space on right for the announcement text.

### Shot 8 — Final hold (28.0 – 30.0s)
Same subject, confident still pose. Space at bottom for promo code.

## Audio direction

- No dialogue. Subject breathing prominent in running + gym shots.
- Music: cinematic, minimal, slow build. BPM ~70. Low brass pad or
  soft piano + sub bass. Single sustained swell into brand reveal.
- Sound design: wind on run, barbell clack on lift, soft whoosh on
  tee match-cut, vinyl warmth throughout.
- Mix: –14 LUFS for social.

Royalty-free starting points: Musicbed "Stillness", Artlist
"Becoming", Epidemic Sound "First Light".

## Color & finish

- LUT: Kodak 2383 or FilmConvert Kodak 2393 at 50%
- Highlights: rolled off, no clipping
- Shadows: lifted slightly, cool +5 cyan
- Skin: protected, warm
- Film grain: 35mm, strength 15–20%
- Vignette: subtle, 10%
- Letterboxing: none (full 9:16)
